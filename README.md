# Poison Arrow

## Overview

Poison Arrow is my professional cybersecurity portfolio.

This site serves as a centralized platform to document:

- Certifications earned
- Technical labs completed or in progress
- Scripts and tools developed
- Detection engineering exercises
- Research and articles of interest

The goal is to maintain a living record of my technical development, practical experience, and continued education within cybersecurity.
